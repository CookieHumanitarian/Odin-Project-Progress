Celery Victor

Description

Craving a healthier onion ring? You're in the right place!

Ingredients

  • Celery
  • Water
  • Bouillon cube
  • Italian salad dressing
  • Pimiento strips

Steps

  1. Trim root end off celery but do not separate stalks. Remove leaves and coarse outer stalks
  2. Cut celery bunch crosswise once so bottom section is 5 inches long. Cut bottom section crosswise into quarter; tie quarters with string
  3. In skillet, heat water to boiling; dissolve bouillon cube in water. Add celery bundles
  4. Cover; heat to boiling. Cook about 15 minutes. Drain celery; place in shallow glass dish
  5. Pour salad dressing over celery. Refrigerate 3 hours, turning bundles twice
  6. To serve, place a bundle cut side down on each salad plate; remove string. Top with pimiento strips

Makes 4 servings
